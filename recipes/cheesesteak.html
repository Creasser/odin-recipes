Cheese Steak Sando Recipe

Description

This is the recipe for a classic Cheese Steak Sandwhich. This recipe will walk you through the whole process of making a Cheese Steak Sandwhich

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of steak
  • 1 large yellow onion
  • Kikkoman Soysauce
  • Fresh rolls
  • 1 pound of white american cheese
  • Garlic and onion powder

Steps

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat with butter inside the pan
  2. Add onions to the pan and cook them until they are clear
  3. Slice the steak into thin pieces
  4. Season the steak with garlic and onion powder. Add soysauce and allow it to marinate for 15 minutes
  5. Add steak to pan and cook to perfered temperature
  6. Once steak is done, add the onions back to the pan
  7. Cut rolls in half. Add cheese to each side and toast until cheese is melted
  8. Add steak and onions to the bun and enjoy!
