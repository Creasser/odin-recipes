Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe

Description

Alfredo is a pasta dish made from fettuccine pasta tossed with Parmesan cheese and butter. As the cheese melts, it emulsifies the liquids to form a smooth and rich coating on the pasta.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 stick butter
  • Freshly grated parmesan
  • Salt and pepper
  • Chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Boil large pot of water with salt added
  2. Add pasta to water and cook. Save 1 cup of pasta water and drain the rest
  3. Put a large skillet over medium heat. Add cream and butter to the skillet. Whisk in parmesan and add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Let sauce simmer for 1 to 2 minutes
  5. Add cooked pasta to the sauce. Add pasta water slowly to reduce the thickness of the sauce. Garnish with parsley and enjoy!
